Pushing data into the database

From OIFits files...

Calibrated OIFITS (L2) Published calibrated OIFITS (L3)
OIFits conformance is reported during the submission process. OIVAL also splits in granules and reports errors as a separeted tool. Enhancements for the validation have been identified but not yet planned.

From metadata file...

The user can also choose to process his observations locally and upload only their metadata by submitting a file of metadata in XML format.

A wiki page is documenting the file format. See also ticket #550 - Upload of preprocessed metadata (L0, L2, L3).

From a collection of OIFits files on VizieR...

The VizieR database contains catalogs of observations associated with paper. Given the id of such a catalog, the service can retrieve the files and other data from the associated catalog description. See also ticket #549 - Permit upload of OIFits L3 collection attached to article.

For example catalog named J/A+A/545/A130.

From an external database/service...

The service can retrieve data from other databases and services. Please have a look onto the various data sources

See ticket #557 - Provide system to upload from external databases and this wiki page.
