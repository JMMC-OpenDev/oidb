Welcome on the second version of the public release of OiDB !

OiDB aims to centralise the access to reduced optical interferometry data and observation logs obtained with a wide range of interferometers.
We hope that you will find data that is useful for your research, share yours and provide us with feedback to precise the future roadmap.

Look at the quick OiDB's introduction 7 min video !

If OiDB is useful to your research, please cite it in your publications by adding the following sentence in the acknowledgement section:

"This research has made use of the Jean-Marie Mariotti Center OiDB service available at http://oidb.jmmc.fr ."
