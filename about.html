OiDB: A global database for optical interferometry

OiDB is a service developed by the JMMC to provide astronomers with a convenient solution to query calibrated optical interferometry data (OIFITS format) as well as regularly-updated observation logs obtained with a wide range of interferometric instruments. It relies on Virtual Observatory standards and tools to increase diffusion and operability. Scientific and technical background of OiDb are described in the paper Haubois et al., 2014, SPIE”.

OiDb is currently in development and this version is a prototype. User’s feedback are very welcome.
